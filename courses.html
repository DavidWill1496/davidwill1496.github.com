Relevant courses and descriptions

The following information contains main topics of the most relevant subjects studied during the degree of Mechatronics Engineering in Nueva Granada Military University

Some tools used in this course.

FESTO: Pneumatic and Hydraulic equipment such as cylinders, valves, switches, Fluidsim simulation software
LabVolt by Festo: Motors and Dynamometer
YASKAWA: Servomotor and driver
Proteus: Simulation software for circuits, PCB design
C++: Programming language for the embedded system, PWM Module, DAC (Digital-Analog Converter C++)
STM: Embedded system for programming solutions (C++)
